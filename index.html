SKY: Children of the Light

A journey about light, friendship, and memories.

About Sky

Sky: Children of the Light is a social adventure game developed by thatgamecompany, the award-winning studio behind Journey and Flower. Released in 2019, the game allows players to explore magical realms, relive the memories of Spirits, and connect with people from all around the world.
While Sky is known for its beautiful visuals and relaxing gameplay, what makes it truly special is the friendships formed along the way. What began as a simple game for me eventually became a place filled with kindness, unforgettable adventures, and some of the most meaningful connections in my life.

The Seven Realms of Sky

Each realm tells a different story, offering unique landscapes, challenges, and memories waiting to be discovered.

Isle of Dawn

“Where every journey begins.”

The first realm of Sky introduces players to the basics of flight and exploration. Its vast desert landscape symbolizes the beginning of every Child of Light’s adventure.

Daylight Prairie

“A land of freedom and endless skies.”

Filled with green fields, floating islands, and gentle creatures, Daylight Prairie encourages players to soar through the skies and experience the joy of exploration.

Hidden Forest

“A mysterious forest touched by rain.”

Hidden Forest is known for its tranquil atmosphere and constant rainfall. Beneath its peaceful appearance lies a realm full of secrets waiting to be uncovered.

Valley of Triumph

“A celebration of speed and achievement.”

This snowy realm challenges players with races and exciting adventures while showcasing breathtaking mountain scenery.

Golden Wasteland

“Courage shines brightest in darkness.”

Unlike the previous realms, Golden Wasteland presents danger and uncertainty. Players must navigate through ruins while avoiding the terrifying Dark Dragons.

Explore Realm →

Vault of Knowledge

“A realm devoted to wisdom and discovery.”

Filled with ancient knowledge and celestial mysteries, Vault of Knowledge encourages players to uncover the history of the fallen kingdom.

Eye of Eden

“The final journey and ultimate sacrifice.”

Eye of Eden serves as the culmination of every Sky adventure. Here, players face their greatest challenge and learn the true meaning of giving light to others.
