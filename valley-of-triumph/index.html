Valley of Triumph

“Race down the ridge, and the city gates will open to honor you.”

Valley of Triumph is the fourth realm in Sky, a breathtaking kingdom of snow-covered landscapes, towering monuments, and echoes of past glory. After journeying through the rain-soaked paths of Hidden Forest, players arrive in a realm filled with speed, light, and celebration. Known for its races, grand architecture, and spectacular views, Valley of Triumph invites players to glide through the skies, skate across frozen lakes, and follow the legacy of a civilization that once celebrated achievement and unity.

The Elders of Valley

Unlike other realms, Valley of Triumph is guided by two Elders who reside within the Valley Temple at the Coliseum. After reliving their memory, players can awaken the spirits they have discovered throughout the realm and unlock the path toward Golden Wasteland.

The twin Elders symbolize the competitive yet harmonious spirit that defines Valley of Triumph. Their presence reflects the realm’s themes of perseverance, achievement, and celebration.

Places to Discover

Valley Rest

The welcoming entrance to the realm. Here, players can rest beside a campfire before beginning their journey down the snowy slopes ahead.

Village of Dreams

A charming winter village introduced during the Season of Dreams. This peaceful community tells the story of a young skater pursuing their dream of performing in the grand Coliseum.

Harmony Hall

A gathering place for music lovers throughout Sky. Players can experiment with various instruments and enjoy the musical side of the kingdom.

Hermit Valley

Hidden high above the Village of Dreams, Hermit Valley offers stunning mountain scenery and tells the story of the gentle Bearhug Hermit.

Village Theater

A stage where imagination comes to life. Players can perform, create shows, and relive stories introduced during the Season of Performance.

Frozen Lake

A serene frozen landscape where players can skate across the ice, discover hidden areas, and choose between multiple paths deeper into the realm.

The Citadel

One of the most iconic locations in Valley of Triumph. Floating among the clouds, the Citadel’s towering structures provide a perfect place for players to practice flying and explore the skies.

Lower Valley Track

A fast-paced downhill race filled with obstacles, light fragments, and exhilarating moments as players slide toward the Coliseum.

Upper Valley Track

Aerial racing at its finest. Players soar through clouds, arches, and floating structures while collecting light fragments along the way.

The Coliseum

The heart of Valley of Triumph. This magnificent arena celebrates the achievements of travelers who complete the realm’s races and serves as the gateway to the Valley Temple.

Valley of Triumph is the first realm to introduce large-scale races, allowing players to experience both ground-based and aerial challenges. The realm is also unique for having two Elders instead of one, reflecting the dual paths that define the Valley experience. Over time, Valley of Triumph expanded with several beloved seasonal locations, including the Village of Dreams, Harmony Hall, and Village Theater. Many players consider the Citadel and Coliseum among the most visually impressive locations in Sky due to their monumental architecture and breathtaking views above the clouds.

Like many realms in Sky, Valley of Triumph contains numerous Out of Bounds (OOB) locations discovered by the player community. These hidden spaces often reveal unusual perspectives of familiar landmarks and showcase parts of the realm that normally remain unseen.

Some well-known Valley OOBs include:

  • Above Boats toward Hermit Valley
  • The Dark Sky Above the Coliseum
  • Underneath the Village of Dreams
  • Above the Upper Valley Track / Cloud City
  • Starfield Memorial / Coliseum
  • Glitch Palace

These locations have become popular destinations among OOB explorers who enjoy uncovering hidden corners of the kingdom beyond the intended map boundaries.
