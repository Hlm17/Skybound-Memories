The Story

Inspired by Community Lore

The Beginning

Long before the Kingdom of Sky fell into silence, the world was sustained by two opposing forces: Light and Void. Light represented life, hope, and connection, while the Void embodied isolation and corruption. Together, they maintained the balance that allowed the kingdom to flourish.

According to the lore, Light flowed naturally across the skies through countless living beings and luminous creatures, creating an endless cycle that connected every corner of the kingdom.

The Cycle of Light

Light was never meant to remain still.

It descended into the world as falling stars, scattered across the land as tiny fragments, and gave birth to Light Creatures that carried its energy throughout the realms. When their journey was complete, that Light returned to the Eye of Eden, ascended through Orbit, and was reborn once again as new stars.

This eternal cycle became the foundation of life in Sky, where every journey begins with Light and eventually returns to it.

The Rise of the Kingdom

As civilization grew, seven Elders and a mysterious ruler known by the community as King Resh discovered that Light could be harnessed as a powerful source of energy. Their discoveries allowed the Kingdom of Sky to prosper, building magnificent cities, advanced technology, and the seven realms we explore today.

Each Elder became the guardian of a realm, preserving its purpose while helping maintain the balance of the kingdom.

The Fall

Prosperity did not last forever.

As the kingdom relied more heavily on Light, corruption began to spread. The mysterious Void twisted creatures, darkened the environment, and transformed once peaceful beings into the Dark Dragons, Dark Crabs, and other corrupted lifeforms found throughout Sky.

Eventually, the once-thriving civilization collapsed, leaving behind only ruins, forgotten memories, and Spirits waiting to be remembered.

A New Journey

Players enter this world as Children of the Light, continuing the cycle that has existed for ages.

By restoring Spirits, guiding lost Light back to the heavens, and ultimately sacrificing themselves in the Eye of Eden, they help keep the cycle of Light alive once more. Every rebirth is not an ending, but the beginning of another journey, carrying hope to a kingdom that still remembers the Light.

Lore Credits
This story is inspired by Sky Lore Theories written by Amity206 (2023), a community interpretation based on official Sky lore, The Art of Sky, concept art, and developer commentary. Some details remain speculative and are not part of the official narrative.
